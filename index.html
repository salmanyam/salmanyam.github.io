Reserach Interests

I am broadly interested in researching system security. More specifically, I assess the threat intelligence of multi-stage advanced attacks, assess defense mechanisms to find loopholes in system defenses and extract exploit-patterns to make a unified framework to describe various attacks. I also conduct data-driven security to ensure the integrity of computer systems. Broadly, my research falls under the following areas:

  • Threat Intelligence Analysis
  • Defense Analysis
  • Security of embedded systems
  • Data-driven security
  • Security in connected vehicle technology
