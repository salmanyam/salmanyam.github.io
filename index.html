I am a fourth-year Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Computer Science at Virginia Tech working under the supervision of Prof. Daphne Yao and a member of Yao Group. As a member of Yao Group, I am focused on developing deployable tools with concrete methodologies and metrics to quantify and measure security assurance. Prior to starting my Ph.D. at Virginia Tech, I worked for the automation and security of connected vehicle technology and development of platform tools for embedded (e.g., smartwatches) and mobile devices.

Before joining graduate school, I obtained my bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (2013). I also worked as both Software and Senior Software Engineer at Samsung R&D.
